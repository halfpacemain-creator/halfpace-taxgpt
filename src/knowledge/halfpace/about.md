HalfPace TaxGPT is an AI assistant by HalfPace built to give Indian individuals, professionals and businesses clear, jargon-free guidance on:

- Income Tax, GST, TDS / TCS
- ROC / MCA, LLP, Trademark
- Accounting, Bookkeeping, Payroll, Professional Tax
- MSME / Udyam, Startup India, business compliance

It is **informational** and does not replace a Chartered Accountant, Company Secretary, or legal counsel. For notices, penalties, prosecution, cross-border matters or any complex restructuring, always consult a qualified professional.
