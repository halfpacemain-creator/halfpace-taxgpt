**Conditions to claim ITC (Section 16(2)):**
1. Possession of tax invoice / debit note.
2. Receipt of goods or services.
3. Supplier has uploaded the invoice (auto-populated in GSTR-2B) AND paid tax to government.
4. Recipient has filed return u/s 39 (GSTR-3B).
5. Payment to supplier within **180 days** from invoice (else reverse ITC with interest; can reclaim on payment).

**Time limit (Section 16(4)):** ITC for an invoice / debit note pertaining to an FY may be claimed **up to 30 November** of the next FY or filing of the annual return, whichever is earlier.

**Blocked credits (Section 17(5)) — illustrative:**
- Motor vehicles (seating ≤ 13) except for resale, passenger transport, driving school
- Food & beverages, outdoor catering, club / health / fitness memberships
- Works contract for immovable property (except plant & machinery, or where output is works contract)
- Goods / services for personal consumption
- Goods lost, stolen, destroyed, written off or disposed by way of gift / free samples
- CSR expenditure (w.e.f. 1 Jan 2023)
