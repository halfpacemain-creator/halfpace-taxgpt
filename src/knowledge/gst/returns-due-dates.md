| Return | Frequency | Due date |
| --- | --- | --- |
| GSTR-1 | Monthly | 11th of next month |
| GSTR-1 (QRMP) | Quarterly | 13th of month after quarter |
| IFF (QRMP) | First 2 months | 13th of next month |
| GSTR-3B | Monthly | 20th of next month |
| GSTR-3B (QRMP) | Quarterly | 22nd / 24th of month after quarter (state-wise) |
| CMP-08 | Quarterly (composition) | 18th of month after quarter |
| GSTR-4 | Annual (composition) | 30 June of next FY |
| GSTR-9 | Annual | 31 December of next FY |
| GSTR-9C | Reconciliation (turnover > ₹5 cr) | 31 December of next FY |

**Late fees (Section 47):**
- GSTR-3B / GSTR-1 nil return: ₹20/day (₹10 CGST + ₹10 SGST), capped at ₹500.
- GSTR-3B / GSTR-1 with liability: ₹50/day (₹25 + ₹25), capped slab-wise by turnover.
- GSTR-9: ₹200/day (₹100 + ₹100), capped at 0.25% of turnover in the state.

**Interest:** 18% p.a. on net cash tax liability (s.50(1)); 24% p.a. on wrongly availed and utilised ITC (s.50(3)).
