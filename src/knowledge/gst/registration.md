Registration is mandatory once **aggregate turnover** (PAN-India, all GSTINs) exceeds the threshold:

| Supply type | Normal states | Special category states |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Exclusive supply of goods | ₹40 lakh | ₹20 lakh |
| Services (or mixed) | ₹20 lakh | ₹10 lakh |

Special category states: Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Tripura (₹10 lakh for both goods & services); Arunachal Pradesh, Meghalaya, Sikkim, Uttarakhand, Puducherry, Telangana (₹20 lakh services).

**Compulsory registration regardless of turnover (Section 24):**
- Inter-state taxable supply of goods
- Casual / non-resident taxable persons
- Persons liable to pay under RCM
- E-commerce operators and sellers on ECO
- Input Service Distributors
- TDS / TCS deductors

Composition scheme limits: ₹1.5 crore for goods (₹75 lakh for special category) and ₹50 lakh for services (s.10(2A)).
