| Section | Nature of payment | Rate | Threshold |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 192 | Salary | As per slab | Basic exemption |
| 194A | Interest (other than securities) | 10% | ₹40,000 (₹50,000 senior) bank; ₹5,000 others |
| 194C | Contractor | 1% (Ind/HUF) / 2% (others) | ₹30,000 single / ₹1,00,000 aggregate |
| 194H | Commission / Brokerage | **2%** (w.e.f. 1 Oct 2024; earlier 5%) | ₹15,000 |
| 194I | Rent — land / building | 10% | ₹2,40,000 |
| 194I | Rent — plant & machinery | 2% | ₹2,40,000 |
| 194J | Professional fees | 10% (2% for technical / royalty for cinema) | ₹30,000 |
| 194Q | Purchase of goods | 0.1% | > ₹50 lakh (buyer's turnover > ₹10 cr) |
| 194O | E-commerce sale of goods / services | **0.1%** (w.e.f. 1 Oct 2024; earlier 1%) | ₹5 lakh |
| 194T | Firm → Partner (salary / remuneration / interest) | 10% | ₹20,000 (w.e.f. 1 Apr 2025) |

**TDS deposit:** by 7th of next month (April: 30 April).
**TDS returns:** quarterly — 31 Jul, 31 Oct, 31 Jan, 31 May.
**Interest (s.201(1A)):** 1%/month for late deduction; 1.5%/month for late deposit.

If PAN is not furnished, TDS at higher of (i) twice the prescribed rate, (ii) 20% (Section 206AA).
