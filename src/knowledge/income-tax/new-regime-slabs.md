The new tax regime under Section 115BAC is the **default** for individuals, HUFs, AOPs (other than co-operative societies), BOIs and AJPs from AY 2024-25 onwards.

**Slabs (AY 2025-26):**

| Total Income | Rate |
| --- | --- |
| Up to ₹3,00,000 | Nil |
| ₹3,00,001 – ₹7,00,000 | 5% |
| ₹7,00,001 – ₹10,00,000 | 10% |
| ₹10,00,001 – ₹12,00,000 | 15% |
| ₹12,00,001 – ₹15,00,000 | 20% |
| Above ₹15,00,000 | 30% |

- Standard deduction for salaried / pensioners: **₹75,000**
- Rebate under Section 87A: full rebate if total income ≤ **₹7,00,000** (max ₹25,000); marginal relief applies just above this limit.
- Surcharge capped at **25%** (vs 37% in old regime).
- Health & Education Cess: **4%** of (tax + surcharge).

To opt OUT (use old regime) where the taxpayer has business / professional income, file **Form 10-IEA** before the due date u/s 139(1).
