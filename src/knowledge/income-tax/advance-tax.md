Advance tax applies if estimated tax liability for the year is **₹10,000 or more** (Section 208). Senior citizens (≥60) without business income are exempt.

**Instalments (Section 211):**

| Due date | Cumulative % of tax payable |
| --- | --- |
| 15 June | 15% |
| 15 September | 45% |
| 15 December | 75% |
| 15 March | 100% |

Presumptive taxpayers under s.44AD / 44ADA pay 100% by **15 March**.

**Interest:**
- **Section 234B** — 1% per month if advance tax paid < 90% of assessed tax; from 1 April of AY till payment.
- **Section 234C** — 1% per month for shortfall in any instalment (3 months for first three instalments; 1 month for the March instalment).
