**Income-tax (Section 32 + Rule 5):**
- Method: **Written Down Value (WDV)** on **block of assets** (except power generation, which may use SLM).
- Common rates: Buildings — 10% (residential 5%); Furniture — 10%; Plant & Machinery — 15%; Computers & software — 40%; Motor vehicles — 15% (30% for commercial use).
- Additional depreciation @ 20% on new plant & machinery (manufacturing) in year of put-to-use.

**Companies Act (Schedule II):**
- Based on **useful life** of the asset; entity may use SLM or WDV.
- Common useful lives: General plant & machinery — 15 years; Computers — 3 years (servers 6); Office equipment — 5 years; Buildings (RCC) — 60 years.

Differences between the two systems give rise to **deferred tax** under Ind AS 12 / AS 22.
