| Form | Purpose | Due date |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AOC-4 | Financial statements | Within 30 days of AGM |
| MGT-7 / MGT-7A (OPC, small co.) | Annual return | Within 60 days of AGM |
| ADT-1 | Auditor appointment | Within 15 days of AGM |
| DIR-3 KYC | Annual DIN KYC | 30 September |
| DPT-3 | Return of deposits / exempt loans | 30 June |
| MSME-1 | Half-yearly dues to MSME suppliers | 30 April & 31 October |

AGM must be held within 6 months of FY end (15 months from previous AGM); first AGM within 9 months of first FY end. Late filing attracts ₹100 per day per form with no cap.
